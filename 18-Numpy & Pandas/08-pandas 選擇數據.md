## Pandas indexing

* 選擇單一"行"數據
```python
dates = pd.date_range('20130101', periods=6)
df = pd.DataFrame(np.arange(24).reshape((6,4)),index=dates, columns=['A','B','C','D'])

"""
             A   B   C   D
2013-01-01   0   1   2   3
2013-01-02   4   5   6   7
2013-01-03   8   9  10  11
2013-01-04  12  13  14  15
2013-01-05  16  17  18  19
2013-01-06  20  21  22  23
"""

print(df['A']) # 只能輸入行index(列不行)
print(df.A)    # 同義

"""
2013-01-01     0
2013-01-02     4
2013-01-03     8
2013-01-04    12
2013-01-05    16
2013-01-06    20
Freq: D, Name: A, dtype: int64
"""
```

* 選擇"多列"數據
```python
# 多列
print(df[0:3])

"""
            A  B   C   D
2013-01-01  0  1   2   3
2013-01-02  4  5   6   7
2013-01-03  8  9  10  11
"""
# 註 : df[3:3]将会是一个空对象


# 多列
print(df['20130102':'20130104'])

"""
A   B   C   D
2013-01-02   4   5   6   7
2013-01-03   8   9  10  11
2013-01-04  12  13  14  15
"""
```

* 標籤loc : 可指定行列範圍 (select by label)
```python
# 指定列標籤
print(df.loc['20130102'])
"""
A    4
B    5
C    6
D    7
Name: 2013-01-02 00:00:00, dtype: int64
"""

# 指定行標籤
print(df.loc[:,['A','B']]) 
"""
             A   B
2013-01-01   0   1
2013-01-02   4   5
2013-01-03   8   9
2013-01-04  12  13
2013-01-05  16  17
2013-01-06  20  21
"""

# 指定列、行標籤
print(df.loc['20130102',['A','B']])
# print(df.loc[:,['A','B']]) # 選擇所有列 以及A、B行
"""
A    4
B    5
Name: 2013-01-02 00:00:00, dtype: int64
"""
```

* 位置iloc : 可指定行列範圍 (select by position)
```python
# Usage : df.iloc[列,行]
print(df.iloc[3,1])
# 13

print(df.iloc[3:5,1:3])
"""
             B   C
2013-01-04  13  14
2013-01-05  17  18
"""

print(df.iloc[[1,3,5],1:3])
"""
             B   C
2013-01-02   5   6
2013-01-04  13  14
2013-01-06  21  22

"""
```

* 結合標籤 + 位置 ix : mixed selection
```python

print(df.ix[:3,['A','C']])
print(df.ix[0:3,['A','C']]) # 同義 :3 和 0:3 一樣意思
"""
            A   C
2013-01-01  0   2
2013-01-02  4   6
2013-01-03  8  10
"""
```

* 判斷式選取 :

print(df[df.A>8])
